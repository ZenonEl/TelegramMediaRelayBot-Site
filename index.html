This is a powerful Telegram bot that allows you to download and forward media files from various platforms such as YouTube, Reddit, TikTok, and others. With its help, you can automate the process of downloading and forwarding media between your contacts, saving time and effort.

Multi-platform support

The project uses powerful tools - yt-dlp and gallery-dl to download media files from platforms like YouTube, Reddit, TikTok and many others. Thanks to integration with yt-dlp/gallery-dl, the bot provides stable and fast content downloading, supporting a wide range of sources and formats.

Forwarding to contacts

The bot can automatically forward downloaded media files to specified contacts or even entire groups, making it easy to share content with friends, colleagues or communities. This is especially useful if someone can't view the content directly by following your link for any reason. Set up a recipient list and the bot will send them media automatically, saving your time and simplifying the media sharing process.

Proxy support

The bot supports working through proxies, including Tor, which ensures anonymity and allows bypassing restrictions imposed by platforms. This is especially useful for users who value privacy and want to protect their confidentiality.

Early development stage

The project is in the active development stage, but it already offers stable basic functionality. I am constantly working on improving and adding new features to make the bot even more convenient and powerful.

Cross-platform

The bot is designed for Linux, but thanks to the use of .NET Core, it can be adapted to work on Windows and macOS. This makes it a flexible solution for various platforms.

Install .NET 8.0+, MySQL 8.0+ and yt-dlp. Download the bot from GitHub, create appsettings.json with your Bot Token from BotFather and database settings. After startup, use /start and exchange unique links with contacts to create your media network.

The bot supports 1000+ sites through yt-dlp and gallery-dl: YouTube, Reddit, TikTok, Pinterest, VK, Telegram, Twitch and many others. This solves the problem when your friends aren't registered on certain platforms or don't want to click links.
